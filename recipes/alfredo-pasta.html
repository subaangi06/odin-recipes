Alfredo Pasta

Home

Description

A rich and velvety white sauce made with butter, heavy cream, and freshly grated Parmesan cheese. It is a quick, 15-minute classic that delivers a smooth, savory, and restaurant-quality finish to any pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 1lb Fettuccine
  • Butter: 6-8 tablespoons, unsalted and cubed
  • Cheese: 1 to 2 cups finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Starch: ~1cup reserved starchy pasta water

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Emulsify
  3. Toss
  4. Adjust
