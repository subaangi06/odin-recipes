Description

Here is a straightforward recipe for a classic homemade Margherita pizza, complete with instructions for the dough, sauce, and baking process.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 300g strong bread flour (or all-purpose flour)
  • 1tsp instant yeast
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1tstp olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 200ml warm water

For the sauce:

  • Passata (or pureed tomatoes, about 200g per pizza)
  • Fresh basil leaves (or 1tsp dried Italian Herbs)
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • salt and pepper to taste

For the topping:

  • Mozzarella cheese, grated (about 100g per pizza)
  • Sliced tomatoes (optional)
  • Extra virgin olive oil for drizzling

Steps:

  1. Make the dough
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Assemble and bake the pizza
