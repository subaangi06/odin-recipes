Description

Lasagne is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of rich meat sauce (ragù), creamy white sauce (béchamel), and pasta sheets.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce (Ragù):

  • 500g ground beef (or a beef and pork mix)
  • 1 onion (finely diced)
  • 2 garlic cloves (crushed)
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery stick (finely diced)
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2tbsp tomato paste
  • 150ml red wine (optional)
  • 1tsp dried oregano or basil

For the White Sauce (Béchamel)

  • 50g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 750ml milk
  • A pinch of ground nutmeg

For the pasta and topping:

  • 250g-300g lasagne sheets (fresh or dried)
  • 100g grated parmesan or cheddar cheese
  • 125 mozzarella

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Ragù
  2. Make the Béchamel
  3. Assemble the Lasagne
  4. Bake
  5. Rest
